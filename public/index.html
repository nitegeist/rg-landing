Our Story

From rebels to builders of the sovereign internet, RaidGuild was born in the fires of decentralization to slay Moloch and create tools and systems that return power to the people.

the spark

2019

Raid Guild was founded at ETHDenver by a group of passionate rebels to support the DAOHaus ecosystem.

Expanding the Arsenal

2020

Raid Guild launched WrapETH, enabling one-click wrapping/unwrapping of ETH. Our first Public Goods project of many.

Smart Escrow Launch

2020

Raid Guild built SmartInvoice, a milestone-based escrow system for trustless payments for Web3 freelancers and DAOs.

Strengthening the Guild

2021

The first cohort introduced a structured onboarding and new member education process.

Aligning forces

2022

The birth of $RAID token established an alignment mechanism for both clients and guild members.

Rebranding

2025

The fight against Moloch never ends. To stay ahead, we rebranded and refined our services. We remain committed to building public goods, strengthening DAOs and pushing Web3 into a permissionless future.
